Data Attacks and Trends

Investigate the evolving threat landscape, benchmark attack patterns, and coordinate response strategies that minimize business impact.

Overview

Unit IV equips learners to recognise common data breach tactics, interpret telemetry, and strengthen organisational readiness. By pairing audits with response planning, teams learn to anticipate emerging attack trends and implement controls that blunt their effectiveness.

Key Topics

  • Threat intelligence fundamentals and tracking adversary behaviour.
  • Privacy impact assessments and readiness reviews for critical systems.
  • Data loss prevention strategies and continuous monitoring practices.
  • Incident response planning, tabletop exercises, and post-incident retrospectives.

Practice Pathway

  1. Review recent breaches to identify tactics, techniques, and procedures.
  2. Conduct a privacy impact assessment to uncover sensitive data exposure.
  3. Simulate a breach scenario to test communication, containment, and recovery steps.
  4. Analyse monitoring data to surface anomalies and prioritise mitigation actions.
