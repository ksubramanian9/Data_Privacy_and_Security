Learning Goals

  • Understand CIA Triad, regulatory frameworks, and foundational privacy principles.
  • Gain competence with cryptographic techniques for secure communications.
  • Analyse risks, identify vulnerabilities, and recommend mitigation strategies.
  • Explore emerging trends and innovations in privacy-enhancing technologies.

Course Objectives

Fundamentals & Frameworks

Grasp fundamental data privacy and security concepts, including the CIA Triad, privacy-by-design strategies, and real-world policy implications.

Cryptographic Foundations

Develop a working knowledge of cryptographic mechanisms, authentication techniques, and secure communication protocols.

Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Learn to identify threats, assess vulnerabilities, and implement strategies that mitigate security incidents and privacy breaches.

Emerging Trends

Investigate emerging technologies, privacy-enhancing innovations, and adaptive security controls for evolving digital ecosystems.

Learning Units & Resources

Each unit is organized around key concepts, recommended reading, and active learning tasks. Use the quick links to access curated resources and activity ideas.

UNIT I

Introduction to Data Privacy and Security

Overview of privacy frameworks, documentation practices, and the CIA Triad in real-world contexts.

Curated Resources

  • Primer: Privacy-by-Design & Policy Landscape
  • Case Study: Documenting Data Flows for Compliance
  • Interactive: CIA Triad Simulation

Learning Activities

  • Map a data lifecycle for a chosen application and identify privacy touchpoints.
  • Analyse a privacy policy to highlight coverage of confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
UNIT II

Encryption Techniques

Covers symmetric/asymmetric encryption, cryptographic algorithms, and secure key management practices.

Curated Resources

  • Video Lecture: Symmetric vs Asymmetric Encryption
  • Lab Guide: Implementing AES & RSA in Python
  • Reference: Hashing Algorithms Cheat Sheet

Learning Activities

  • Decode a secure message using provided key pairs and validate integrity with hashing.
  • Evaluate key management strategies for a multi-tenant application.
UNIT III

Access Control & Authentication

Explores hashing, digital signatures, multi-factor authentication, and modern access control models.

Curated Resources

  • Guide: Designing RBAC & ABAC Policies
  • Workshop: Building a Secure Authentication Flow
  • Explainer: Digital Signatures & Certificates

Learning Activities

  • Create a threat model for an authentication system with MFA.
  • Compare access control models and present recommendations for a case study.
UNIT IV

Data Audits & Threats

Focus on privacy impact assessments, access control controls, and preparing incident response plans.

Curated Resources

  • Template: Privacy Impact Assessment Checklist
  • Reading: Data Loss Prevention Strategies
  • Scenario: Incident Response Runbook

Learning Activities

  • Run a tabletop exercise to respond to a suspected data breach.
  • Audit sample access logs and identify anomalous activities.
UNIT V

Emerging Trends in Assurance & Security

Delves into lifecycle analysis, adaptive authentication, and contemporary assurance frameworks.

Curated Resources

  • Brief: Identity Lifecycle Management
  • Podcast: Adaptive Authentication in Practice
  • Report: AI for Privacy Assurance

Learning Activities

  • Design an adaptive authentication workflow using contextual signals.
  • Draft a roadmap for integrating privacy-enhancing technologies.
